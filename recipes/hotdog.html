Lasagna

An example of the lasagna you can make following this recipe

Ingredients

  • Hot dogs
  • Hot dog buns
  • American cheese
  • Ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Air fry the hotdogs for 5 minutes in the air fryer
  2. Toast the buns in the air fryer or oven
  3. Put one slice of cheese on the bun, followed by the hotdog
  4. Finish by putting ketchup and mustard on the hotdog
